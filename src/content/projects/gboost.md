## Overview

GBoost is an AI marketing autopilot for local business owners. It turns Google Ads, posts, visuals, and Google Business Profile management into a three-click workflow — replacing fragmented tools and expensive agency retainers with one automated funnel.

## Features

- **AI Google Ads** — AI-written headlines and descriptions, smart keyword and bid suggestions, live preview, and auto-pausing of low performers.
- **AI Posts** — month-long content generated from a single prompt, tone-matched to the business, with one-click approve, edit, or regenerate.
- **AI Review Responses** — on-brand replies to customer reviews, with a queue, history, and response tracking.
- **AI Visuals** — on-brand images generated in ~10 seconds across photo, illustration, and graphic styles, sized for Google posts.
- **Scheduling & calendar** — automated publishing across posts and campaigns.
- **Local SEO & Google Business Profile** — keyword and service optimization with a continuously maintained profile.
- **Insights & offers** — campaign performance tracking and promotional offer management.

## Future Improvements

- Deeper anomaly alerts for ranking and ad-performance drops.
- Expanded template and workflow customization by business type.
- Improved integration observability for API failure diagnostics.
